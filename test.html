Er. Ritesh Goel

Founder

Er.Ritesh Goel graduated from NIT Bhopal as a mechanical engineer. He has more than 9 years of experience in teaching Physics and 6 years of experience in teaching Mechanical Engineering. His expertise in Mechanics,Theory of Machines, Fluid Machanics & Strength of Materials are well known nationwide. He was an Associate Professor at FIITJEE Ltd. for IIt-JEE Physics and has mentored thousands of GATE, UPSC (ESC),IIT-JEE,NEET and AIIMS aspirants in past nine years including many rankers.

Er. Goel is the youngest first-generation academic entrepreneur of Gwalior.He is a renowned poet, street play artist, orator and motivator
